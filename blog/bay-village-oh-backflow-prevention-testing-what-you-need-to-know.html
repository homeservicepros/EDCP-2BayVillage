What is Backflow Prevention and Why is it Important?

Backflow occurs when contaminated water flows backward into your clean water supply. This can happen due to pressure changes in the water system, potentially introducing harmful contaminants into your drinking water. For Bay Village residents, especially those near Rocky River and Westlake, proper backflow prevention is essential for maintaining water quality.

Common Causes of Backflow in Bay Village Homes

  • Sudden changes in water pressure
  • Broken water mains
  • Fire hydrant use
  • Irrigation systems
  • Pool filling systems

When is Backflow Testing Required?

In Bay Village, backflow prevention devices must be tested annually by a certified professional. This requirement helps ensure the safety of our community's water supply. Signs You Need Immediate Backflow Testing

  • Discolored water
  • Unusual taste or odor
  • Low water pressure
  • Visible debris in water

The Testing Process Explained

Our certified technicians follow a thorough testing protocol that includes:

  1. Visual inspection of the device
  2. Testing of all check valves
  3. Verification of relief valve operation
  4. Documentation and certification
