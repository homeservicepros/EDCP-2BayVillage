Common Causes of Kitchen Drain Clogs in Bay Village Homes

  • Grease and cooking oil buildup
  • Food particles and debris
  • Coffee grounds and eggshells
  • Soap scum accumulation
  • Tree root intrusion in older Bay Village properties

Warning Signs Your Kitchen Drain Needs Professional Attention

Don't wait until your drain completely stops working. Watch for these early warning signs:

  • Slow draining water
  • Gurgling sounds
  • Unpleasant odors
  • Water backing up
  • Multiple clogged drains

Preventive Maintenance Tips

Keep your kitchen drain flowing freely with these expert-recommended practices:

  • Install and maintain a quality garbage disposal
  • Use sink strainers to catch food particles
  • Regular professional maintenance checks
  • Proper grease disposal methods

For commercial kitchens, we also offer specialized grease trap cleaning services to prevent serious blockages.

When to Call a Professional

While minor clogs might be resolved with DIY methods (learn more in our article about DIY vs. Professional Drain Cleaning), certain situations require expert attention:

  • Multiple clogged drains
  • Recurring blockages
  • Sewage odors
  • Water backing up into other drains
