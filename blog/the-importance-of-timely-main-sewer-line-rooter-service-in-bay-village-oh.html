Understanding Main Sewer Line Problems in Bay Village

Bay Village's aging infrastructure and mature tree population create unique challenges for homeowners. Root intrusion, particularly from older trees, is one of the most common issues we encounter in local sewer lines. Signs of trouble include:

  • Slow-draining fixtures throughout your home
  • Gurgling sounds from toilets and drains
  • Sewage backups in basement drains
  • Unpleasant odors around your property
  • Wet spots in your yard
